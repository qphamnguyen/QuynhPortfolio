About Me

I am currently a Cognitive Science student at the University of California, San Diego, specializing in Design and Interaction. I want to learn more about wireframing and prototyping digital interfaces, and gain more hands-on experience in designing. I am proficient in using Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop, and have learned HTML, CSS, Excel, Photoshop and Javascript in the past. I am currently learning Python.
In my free time I like to rollerskate for funsies and draw when I feel inspired (and to make my drawing tablet less lonely).

Past Projects

eNovel

eNovel is a mock-site I worked on as part of COGS 3 during winter 2022. It is the homepage concept for a hypothetical reading app.

"eNovel is a site for all readers, from the casual peruser to the avid bookworm. It aims to make reading a fun community experience, featuring many genres and discussion-based forums that allow readers to connect with each other."

Snore

Snore is a Figma prototype of a health app I designed as part of COGS 3, centered around helping users sleep.

"An app for college students, Snore gives its users tips for how to maximize their sleep and which places nearby are best, allowing users to submit review-style posts for their peers."
